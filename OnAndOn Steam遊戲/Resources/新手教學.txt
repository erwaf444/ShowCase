第一點，按下滑鼠左鍵可以蓄力發射
第二點，每一個物體都可以被吸附，但不能被重複連續吸附，吸附物體的時候，不用蓄力就能夠發射
第三點，連續吸附不同物體，發射的力道越遠

First.Pressing the left mouse button allows you to charge and launch.
Second.Every object can be attached, but it cannot be attached consecutively more than once. When attaching an object, you can launch it without charging.
Third.Consecutively attaching different objects increases the launch force.
End.Enjoy!
